Abstract

TTRPGs such as Dungeons & Dragons or Daggerheart can be used for so much more than just an entertaining experience. They offer an opportunity to create a collaborative storytelling experience that can boost a person’s cognitive and personal development.

Works Cited

Stubbs, Richard, and Nikolas Sorensen. 2025. “Tabletop Role-Playing Games and Social and Emotional Learning in School Settings.” Social and Emotional Learning: Research, Practice, and Policy 5 (June): Article 100090. https://doi.org/10.1016/j.sel.2025.100090
